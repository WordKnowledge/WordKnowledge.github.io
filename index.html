Estimate the number of words that you know

How many words do you know in English or another language? You can get a rough idea by filling the form below, using word and page counts from a dictionary. Insofar as the resulting estimate is realistic, it will actually be an estimate of how many words you know from that dictionary, of course! To estimate the number of words you know in a LANGUAGE, you would need an unabridged dictionary supplemented with lists of words that probably would not be in a dictionary, such as proper names that you know that come from places where the language you are testing is not the language of the communities.

Estimate the number of words that you know

    1) Get a dictionary with a language of your choice.

    2) Look at the first word on each of 25 randomly selected pages and
    enter the number of words you know from those 25 in the box below.
    

    3) Enter the total number of words from a randomly selected page.
    

    4) Enter the total number of pages in your dictionary.
    

    5) Hit Submit once you have entered values into all the boxes above.
